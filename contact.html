First Time?

Plan Your Visit

We want your first visit to FGCK Malaba to be special. Here's everything you need to know.

1
📅 Choose Your Service
We recommend Sunday Morning at 10:00 AM for first-time visitors. It's our main service with children's church available concurrently.
2
📍 Find Our Location
We are located along the Malaba–Busia Road in Malaba Town, Busia County. Ask anyone in Malaba for "FGCK Malaba" — we are well known!
3
👗 Come as You Are
There is no dress code at FGCK Malaba. Come in what makes you comfortable. You will be warmly welcomed by our greeters at the door.
4
🙋 Let Us Know You're Coming
WhatsApp us on +254 700 000 000 so we can personally welcome you. We have a special gift for first-time visitors!
5
☕ Stay for Fellowship
After the service, please stay for tea and snacks. It's the best way to meet our congregation and feel at home immediately.
