History

Our Heritage in Kenya

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya (FGCK) was founded in 1949 in Kisumu District by missionaries from Finland. The church grew rapidly across Kenya, driven by a passion for Pentecostal faith and community transformation.

The FGCK Malaba assembly grew from a small fellowship of believers meeting in a humble hall, to a thriving congregation that today serves hundreds of families. Located at the strategic Kenya–Uganda border town, our church has a unique calling to minister to people of diverse backgrounds.

Today, FGCK spans all 47 counties of Kenya with over a million members, 12 regions, 87 districts, and more than 630 local church assemblies. Malaba is proud to be one of the most vibrant assemblies in the Western Region.

Who We Are

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To preach the Full Gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples of all nations, and transform the Malaba community through the power of the Holy Spirit — spiritually, socially, and economically.

👁️

Our Vision

To be a strong and caring fellowship of Spirit-filled believers who are holistically transformed by God's Word, and who actively influence every sphere of life in Malaba and beyond.

💎

Core Values

  • Biblical Authority & Sound Doctrine
  • Holy Spirit Empowerment
  • Authentic Worship & Prayer
  • Discipleship & Spiritual Growth
  • Community Impact & Compassion
  • Family & Generational Legacy
