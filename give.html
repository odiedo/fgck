Why We Give

Sowing Into God's Kingdom

Your generosity powers the ministry of FGCK Malaba — from Sunday services to outreach programs, youth camps, and our new sanctuary project. Every single gift makes an eternal difference.

We believe in radical generosity as a spiritual discipline. Your tithe, offering, and special gifts are sown into good soil and will produce a hundredfold return in your life and in the Kingdom.

"Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this," says the Lord Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it." — Malachi 3:10
"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7

Our Commitment to You

How Your Gift is Used

🏛️
New Sanctuary Project (40%)
Building a permanent, spacious sanctuary that will serve the Malaba community for generations to come.
📖
Weekly Ministry Operations (30%)
Funding Sunday services, Bible studies, sound systems, printing, and pastoral administration.
🌍
Outreach & Community (20%)
Street evangelism, hospital visits, food aid for the vulnerable, and missions in surrounding areas.
👶
Youth & Children (10%)
Funding youth camps, children's church materials, and next-generation discipleship programmes.
