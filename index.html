Our Story

A Church Built on Covenant & Community

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya (FGCK) Malaba is a thriving Pentecostal congregation at the heart of Malaba town. We are part of the wider FGCK family, founded in 1949 by Finnish missionaries — today reaching every county in Kenya with over a million members.

In Malaba, we have grown from a small fellowship into a vibrant community of believers united by a passion for God's Word, dynamic worship, and genuine love for one another.
