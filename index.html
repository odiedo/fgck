Our Story

A Church Built on Faith & Community

Full Gospel Churches of Kenya (FGCK) Malaba is a thriving Pentecostal congregation located at the gateway of Kenya and Uganda. We are part of the wider FGCK family, which was founded in 1949 by Finnish missionaries and today spans every county in Kenya.

In Malaba, we have grown from a small fellowship into a vibrant community of believers united by a passion for God's Word, dynamic worship, and genuine love for one another. Our doors are open to all — newcomers, families, and everyone in between.

We believe in the full gospel: salvation, healing, baptism of the Holy Spirit, and the imminent return of Jesus Christ. We are committed to discipleship, community transformation, and missions.
